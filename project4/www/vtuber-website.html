VTuber Website Design

Project Overview

The VTuber Website Design project was created to meet the unique needs of online creators in the virtual YouTuber (VTuber) space. The goal was to design a vibrant, user-friendly platform to help VTubers showcase their content, connect with fans, and grow their digital presence.

Features

  • Dynamic homepage with an animated banner and featured content section.
  • Integrated schedule for live streams and upcoming events.
  • Custom contact form with fan suggestions and collaboration inquiries.

Design Approach

I focused on a vibrant color palette, clean layouts, and playful typography to align with the VTuber aesthetic. The design incorporated animations, icons, and custom graphics to bring the virtual world to life.

Technologies Used

  • Figma for wireframe and prototype
  • Font Awesome icons for social media links and features.
  • Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator for custom graphics.
