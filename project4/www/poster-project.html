National Park Poster Design

Project Overview

The National Park Poster Design project was inspired by the natural beauty of Voyageurs National Park. The goal was to create a visually striking and minimalist poster that captures the essence of the park’s serene landscapes and wildlife.

Features

  • Minimalist design style emphasizing clean lines and bold typography.
  • Hand-drawn elements inspired by the park's iconic landscapes.
  • Color palette reflecting the natural tones of Voyageurs National Park.
  • Incorporated both physical and digital formats for wide accessibility.

Design Approach

The design focused on evoking an emotional connection to nature through simplicity and harmony. The typography was chosen to complement the visuals without overpowering the artwork. The illustrations were carefully curated to reflect the park’s unique characteristics.

Technologies Used

  • Adobe Illustrator for vector illustrations and typography design.
